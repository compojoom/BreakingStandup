# Privacy Policy for Breaking Standup

*Last updated: March 25, 2026*

Breaking Standup is developed by Daniel Dimitrov (compojoom).

## Data Collection

Breaking Standup does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

## Calendar Access

The app requests access to your calendars solely to detect upcoming meetings and display reminders. Calendar data is read locally using Apple's EventKit framework and is never sent to any server or third party.

## Analytics & Tracking

The app contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not communicate with any external services.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at daniel@compojoom.com.
